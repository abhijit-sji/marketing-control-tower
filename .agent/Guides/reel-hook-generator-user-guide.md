 ## What is the Reel Hook Generator?

 The **Reel Hook Generator** is an AI-powered tool that creates scroll-stopping
 opening lines for your short-form video content (Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube
 Shorts, Facebook Reels).

 ### Why Use It?

 - **Save Time**: Generate 5 high-quality hooks in seconds
 - **Platform-Optimized**: Hooks tailored to each platform's algorithm and audience
 - **Psychology-Driven**: Uses proven viewer psychology principles
 - **Data-Backed**: Based on gold examples from high-performing content
 - **Quality-Scored**: Each hook rated on 4 key metrics (scroll-stop power, clarity,
 emotional pull, specificity)

 ### What Makes a Good Hook?

 A great reel hook:
 - **Stops the scroll** within 1-2 seconds
 - **Is crystal clear** about what the video is about
 - **Triggers emotion** (curiosity, fear, FOMO, excitement)
 - **Is specific** (not generic)

 ## Filling Out the Form

 ### Required Fields ⚠️

 #### 1. **Topic / Main Message**
 What your reel is about. Be specific!

 **✅ Good Examples:**
 - "Your reels fail because you start them wrong"
 - "3 mistakes killing your Instagram reach"
 - "I grew from 0 to 10K followers in 30 days"

 **❌ Bad Examples:**
 - "Instagram tips" (too generic)
 - "Marketing" (too broad)
 - "My video" (not descriptive)

 **Minimum:** 10 characters
 **Tip:** Include the problem, outcome, or hook idea

 ---

 #### 2. **Target Audience**
 Who are you speaking to? Be very specific about their pain points.

 **✅ Good Examples:**
 - "SaaS founders struggling to get demo requests from social media"
 - "Content creators under 10K followers who can't crack the algorithm"
 - "Real estate agents managing 50+ properties without a CRM"

 **❌ Bad Examples:**
 - "Everyone" (too broad)
 - "Business owners" (too generic)
 - "People interested in marketing" (not specific enough)

 **Minimum:** 10 characters
 **Tip:** Include WHO they are + WHAT they struggle with

 ---

 #### 3. **Platform**
 Select where you'll post this reel:

 | Platform | Best For | Avg Attention |
 |----------|----------|---------------|
 | **Instagram Reels** | Visual + text overlay, polished content | 1.5 seconds |
 | **YouTube Shorts** | Slightly longer setups, educational content | 2 seconds |
 | **TikTok** | Pattern interrupts, raw/authentic content | 1 second |
 | **Facebook Reels** | Emotional storytelling, relatable content | 2.5 seconds |

 **Tip:** Each platform has different hook styles. Instagram likes clean text
 overlays, TikTok prefers shock value.

 ---

 #### 4. **Primary Goal**
 What do you want viewers to do?

 | Goal | Description | Best Hook Types |
 |------|-------------|-----------------|
 | **Views** | Maximize reach | Curiosity, pattern interrupt |
 | **Saves** | Provide value | Educational, mistake-focused |
 | **Follows** | Grow your audience | Identity callouts, authority |
 | **Clicks** | Drive traffic | Pain points, FOMO |

 **Tip:** Pick ONE goal. Trying to achieve multiple goals dilutes the hook's
 effectiveness.

 ---

 #### 5. **Tone / Voice**
 How should the hook sound?

 **Examples:**
 - "Bold, direct" → "Stop posting reels like this"
 - "Friendly, casual" → "Here's why your reels aren't getting views"
 - "Authoritative, expert" → "The algorithm changed — here's what you need to know"
 - "Urgent, FOMO" → "Do this before the algorithm updates tomorrow"

 **Tip:** Match your brand's voice. Don't be overly formal if your brand is casual.

 ### Advanced Options (Optional) 🔧

 Click **"Show Advanced Options"** to unlock these settings:

 #### **Hook Length**
 - **Short (1-7 words)**: Best for TikTok, fast-paced content
 - **Medium (8-10 words)**: Balanced, works across platforms
 - **Long (11-12 words)**: More context, better for YouTube Shorts

 ---

 #### **Content Format**
 - **Talking Head**: You speaking directly to camera
 - **B-Roll**: Footage with text overlay
 - **Text Overlay**: Bold text on visual background
 - **Mixed**: Combination of above

 **Why it matters:** Talking head allows for longer hooks, text overlays need to be
 punchier.

 ---

 #### **Urgency Level**
 - **Low**: Evergreen content, no time pressure
 - **Medium**: Some timeliness ("This week I...")
 - **High**: Urgent, time-sensitive ("Do this before...")

 ---

 #### **Creator Persona**
 - **Expert**: You're the authority → "I've been doing this for 10 years"
 - **Peer**: You're a friend → "I tried this so you don't have to"
 - **Entertainer**: Fun, engaging → "You won't believe what happened"
 - **Educator**: Teaching mode → "Here's how to fix..."

 #### **Additional Context (Optional)**

 ## Understanding Your Results

 ### Top Hooks Display

 You'll receive **5 hooks**, ranked by overall quality score.

 #### Hook Card Breakdown

 **1. Category Badge**
 - **Purple (Curiosity)**: Open loops, pattern interrupts
 - **Red (Pain)**: Problem-focused, mistake callouts
 - **Orange (Contrarian)**: Against conventional wisdom
 - **Yellow (Mistake)**: "I wasted X doing Y"
 - **Blue (Identity)**: "If you're a [persona]..."
 - **Pink (Shock)**: Unexpected statements
 - **Green (FOMO)**: Time-sensitive urgency

 **2. Hook Text**
 The actual opening line you'll use (max 12 words).

 **3. Best For**
 Who this hook works best for (e.g., "cold audience, educational content").

 **4. Four Quality Scores**

 | Score | Weight | What It Measures |
 |-------|--------|------------------|
 | **Scroll-Stop** | 40% | Does it stop thumbs mid-scroll? |
 | **Clarity** | 25% | Is it immediately clear what it's about? |
 | **Emotional Pull** | 25% | Does it trigger emotion (curiosity, fear, FOMO)? |
 | **Specificity** | 10% | Is it specific, not generic? |

 **Score Interpretation:**
 - **9.0-10.0**: Excellent (use immediately)
 - **8.0-8.9**: Great (minor tweaks optional)
 - **7.0-7.9**: Good (solid, but test variations)
 - **Below 7.0**: Regenerate (shouldn't happen - AI auto-refines)

 **5. Copy Button**
 Click to copy the hook to your clipboard.

 **6. Reasoning (Optional)**
 Why this hook works for your audience.

 **7. AI Feedback (Optional)**
 Strengths and weaknesses from the AI evaluator.

 ### A/B Test Suggestion

 The AI recommends two hooks to test against each other:

 - **Variant A**: First approach
 - **Variant B**: Alternative approach
 - **Variable**: What's different (e.g., "Question vs Statement", "curiosity vs pain")

 **How to use:**
 1. Post both hooks on separate reels (same day, similar content)
 2. Track performance (views, engagement, saves)
 3. Double down on the winner

 ## Best Practices

 ### ✅ Do's

 1. **Be Specific in Inputs**
    - Generic inputs = generic hooks
    - Specific inputs = targeted, high-quality hooks

 2. **Test Multiple Platforms**
    - Each platform has different best practices
    - Generate separate hooks for Instagram vs TikTok

 3. **Use the A/B Test Feature**
    - Post both variants and measure results
    - Learn what resonates with YOUR audience

 4. **Analyze Competitor Hooks**
    - Use the optional field to study what works
    - AI will create original variations

 5. **Save Your Winners**
    - Keep a list of hooks that performed well
    - Feed them back into "Past Performing Hooks" for future generations

 6. **Iterate Based on Scores**
    - If scroll-stop is low, try a different goal or tone
    - If clarity is low, be more specific in your topic

 ---

 ### ❌ Don'ts

 1. **Don't Use Generic Topics**
    - ❌ "Marketing tips"
    - ✅ "Your cold emails fail because you make this one mistake"

 2. **Don't Target Everyone**
    - ❌ "Anyone interested in business"
    - ✅ "B2B founders struggling to book sales calls"

 3. **Don't Copy Hooks Exactly**
    - The hooks are a starting point
    - Customize to match your exact video content

 4. **Don't Ignore Platform Differences**
    - TikTok hooks ≠ Instagram hooks
    - Generate separately for best results

 5. **Don't Skip the Advanced Options**
    - They help the AI understand context
    - Especially useful: content format, creator persona

 6. **Don't Use All 5 Hooks at Once**
    - Pick the top 2-3
    - Test them across different videos

 ## Tips for High-Quality Hooks

 ### 1. Front-Load Value
 Put the benefit or intrigue in the first 3 words.

 **✅ Good:**
 - "Stop posting reels like this"
 - "Your reels fail because..."
 - "I grew 10K followers by..."

 **❌ Bad:**
 - "In this video I'm going to show you..."
 - "Hey guys, welcome back..."

 ---

 ### 2. Use Pattern Interrupts
 Say something unexpected to stop the scroll.

 **Examples:**
 - "I deleted my account with 100K followers"
 - "Posting daily is destroying your reach"
 - "Hashtags haven't worked since 2022"

 ---

 ### 3. Call Out Your Audience
 Use identity-based hooks for warm audiences.

 **Examples:**
 - "If you're a creator under 10K, watch this"
 - "Founders — this reel strategy is for you"
 - "Real estate agents, stop doing this"

 ---

 ### 4. Leverage Curiosity Gaps
 Open a loop that must be closed.

 **Examples:**
 - "I stopped posting reels for 30 days — here's what happened"
 - "The algorithm changed again and nobody noticed"
 - "This mistake costs you 80% of your views"

 ---

 ### 5. Use Specificity
 Numbers, timeframes, and details build credibility.

 **✅ Specific:**
 - "Your reels get 200 views because of this one mistake"
 - "I wasted 6 months making this reel error"

 **❌ Generic:**
 - "Your reels don't get views"
 - "I made mistakes with reels"

 ---

 ### 6. Match Hook to Content
 Your hook must deliver on the promise.

 **If your hook is:**
 "Your reels fail because you start them wrong"

 **Your video must:**
 Explain HOW they're starting wrong and the RIGHT way to start.

 **Never clickbait!** The AI is configured to avoid false promises.

 ---

 ### 7. Test Emotional Triggers

 Different emotions work for different goals:

 | Emotion | Hook Type | Example |
 |---------|-----------|---------|
 | **Curiosity** | Open loop | "The algorithm changed and nobody noticed" |
 | **Fear** | Pain point | "You're losing followers every time you post" |
 | **FOMO** | Urgency | "Do this before the algorithm updates tomorrow" |
 | **Surprise** | Shock value | "I deleted my 100K follower account" |
 | **Relief** | Solution | "Here's the fix that tripled my reach" |

 ## Troubleshooting

 ### "Topic too short" Error

 **Problem:** Your topic is under 10 characters.

 **Solution:** Be more descriptive.
 - ❌ "Reels tips"
 - ✅ "Your reels fail because you start them wrong"

 ---

 ### "Audience too short" Error

 **Problem:** Your audience description is under 10 characters.

 **Solution:** Be more specific about WHO and WHAT.
 - ❌ "Creators"
 - ✅ "Content creators under 10K followers struggling with reach"

 ---

 ### "Tone required" Error

 **Problem:** You didn't specify a tone.

 **Solution:** Add 1-2 adjectives describing your brand voice.
 - "Bold, direct"
 - "Friendly, casual"
 - "Professional, authoritative"

 ---

 ### Hooks Feel Generic

 **Possible Causes:**
 1. Your topic input was too broad
 2. Your audience description lacks specificity
 3. You didn't use advanced options

 **Solutions:**
 - Be more specific in all inputs
 - Use "Competitor Hooks" to show examples
 - Add "Past Performing Hooks" for your style
 - Fill out "Content Format" and "Creator Persona"

 ---

 ### Low Scroll-Stop Scores

 **Possible Causes:**
 - Topic lacks shock value or curiosity
 - Hook is too safe or expected
 - Wrong goal for the content type

 **Solutions:**
 - Try "Primary Goal: Views" (prioritizes scroll-stop)
 - Use "Urgency Level: High"
 - Test "Shock" or "Contrarian" categories (feedback to admin to adjust gold examples)

 ---

 ### Low Clarity Scores

 **Possible Causes:**
 - Topic is vague or unclear
 - Hook is trying to be too clever

 **Solutions:**
 - Be more direct in your topic input
 - Choose "Clarity" focus if available
 - Simplify your message

 ---

 ### Regeneration Taking Too Long

 **Normal Generation Time:** 10-30 seconds

 **If taking 60+ seconds:**
 - The AI may be auto-refining (quality score below 7.5)
 - This is good! It means the AI is ensuring quality
 - Max 3 attempts, then returns best result

 **If it fails:**
 - Check your internet connection
 - Try again with simpler inputs
 - Contact support if persistent

 ## Quick Reference

 ### Hook Formula Cheat Sheet

 ```
 [Pattern Interrupt] + [Specific Problem] + [Implied Solution]

 Examples:
 "Stop [doing X] — here's why"
 "Your [topic] fails because [specific mistake]"
 "I [surprising outcome] by doing [unexpected action]"
 "If you're [identity], you need to [action]"
 "[Number] [timeframe] [outcome] — here's how"
 ```
